Pele 2.0


Pele 2.0 is a more adaptable version of the original Pele. It features a sharp 60/40 rail allowing it to really dig into the face of the wave. The board remains decently wide under the nose and chest with its wide point a little bit more towards neutral/forward which allows stability and ease of paddling. The deep concave starts in the first third of the board, and contains all the way out the tail. Pele 2.0 also features an optional tail V cut which starts right before the center fin and allows for extra hold in some steeper more hollow surf. The tail V gives the surfer the ability to set a high line and hold in some bigger waves. The board turns very well if you put weight on the back it can really loosen up and transfer water very well.
