Sword


The Sword is sharp, fast, and loose allowing the board to slice through the water with ease. This board is easy to blow tail, sink the rails, and absolutely shred. It is relatively flat through the entire board, with a single concave starting in the middle and extending all the way out the tail. It has a sharp edge starting in the last 16 inches which allows it to pick up speed with ease.
