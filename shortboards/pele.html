Pele

Pele is our rounded pin-tail model featuring a single concave starting in the first 16 inches and extending all the way to the tail. The knifey rails have enough volume to keep you in the wave with a hard edge starting in the last 18 inches. Pele works well in surf from 2-3 ft and handles into about a foot overhead. This board features extra volume under the chest for simplified paddling in order to help make some of those faster waves. Also great in beach breaks, and on point breaks.
