Big Red


Big Red is your old school nose rider. If you want to hang your toes up off the nose, and sit there then this board is for you. It has a single concave in the nose to aid with lift, and to hold you on the face of the wave. There is a strong rocker out the back with a flat wide square tail. It allows you ot hold speed and keep your line. The rails are sharp, but full to help you make it through the flat spots. For sure a nose rider you would want to keep around and ride till your arms fall off rom paddling.
